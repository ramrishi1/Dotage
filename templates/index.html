Our mission

The idea of 'Dotage' started after meeting and hearing experience of people after retirement. Making friends in adult life and espcially in later stages of life becomes hard for many. Dotage originated from this. No one should skip making plans because they are alone. There are many people out there with similair situation. Our idea/mission is to connect them.
